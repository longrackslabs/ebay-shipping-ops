# Longracks Labs — Process Flow

**Before & After Automation — Built Feb 19-22, 2026**

Everything started manual. Here's what we've automated and what's left.

## Selling

| Step | Before (all manual) | After |
|------|---------------------|-------|
| Detect new sale | Check eBay app | **AUTOMATED** |
| Buy shipping label | Buy through eBay Seller Hub | **AUTOMATED** |
| Print label | Download PDF, open, print | **AUTOMATED** |
| Print packing list | Handwritten / skipped | **AUTOMATED** |
| Upload tracking to eBay | Copy/paste in Seller Hub | **AUTOMATED** |

## Fulfillment

| Step | Before (all manual) | After |
|------|---------------------|-------|
| Pick & pack | Manual | Manual (irreducible) |
| Schedule USPS pickup | Didn't exist | **AUTOMATED** (manual trigger) |
| Get package to USPS | Drive to post office (30 min RT) | Bin on porch |
